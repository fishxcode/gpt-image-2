# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or perspective.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while seeking clarity.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and project outcomes.
- Respect maintainers' time and project scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or sensitive user data.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make participation unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, report it through the project's GitHub moderation tools or another maintainer-designated channel. Reports will be reviewed with appropriate confidentiality and care.
